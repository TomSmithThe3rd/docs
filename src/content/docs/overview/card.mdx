**Rank cards** are procedurally generated images that show your progress in
[XP](/overview/xp), [messages sent](/overview/messages), and
[voice minutes logged](/overview/voice). It can be viewed most easily through
the [/rank](/commands/rank) command.

## Customization

In the future, rank cards will be very customizable with
[Engauge Premium](/setup/premium). Stay tuned!
